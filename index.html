About Day2

Transforming the journey of Parkinson's disease management with Day2 – a cutting-edge health tech platform tailored to provide personalized care and unwavering support for individuals living with Parkinson's.

Guided by advanced AI technology, Day2 features an empathetic virtual coach who’s always there to inspire, encourage, and keep you on track. Developed in collaboration with Parkinson’s UK, the nation’s leading Parkinson's charity, Day2 empowers you to take control of your health with tools designed to make every day a little easier.
