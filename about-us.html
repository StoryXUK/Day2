Welcome to Day2

Transforming the journey of Parkinson's disease management with Day2 – a cutting-edge platform tailored to provide personalized care and unwavering support for individuals living with Parkinson's.

Guided by advanced and secure AI technology, Day2 features an empathetic virtual coach who’s always there to inspire, encourage, and keep you on track. Developed in consultation with Parkinson’s UK, the nation’s leading Parkinson's charity, Day2 empowers you to take control of your health with tools designed to make every day a little easier.

Our Manifesto

Day2 is a radical new platform designed to empower people with neuro-degenerative conditions to manage their own wellness outside the confines and limitations of the traditional system. Founded by a Parkinson's patient frustrated with a lack of holistic treatment and a fitness expert with 15 years experience working in this field, they saw the potential of technology to revolutionise and democratise care. Day2 is a community powered content hub giving patients the tools they need to help manage their diagnosis. From online movement classes of all levels to recipes and help with sleep, Day2 rounds out your total care package. Day 2 is more than a website or an app, we have strong ties with local UK communities building teams of trusted professionals such as gyms and trainers, physiotherapy, yoga and meditation as well as local social events.

Feel stronger, eat healthier, connect more, and rest easier. Let’s take on today, together.
